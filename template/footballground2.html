The Turf started in July '21 and it is a box cricket and football venue, located at Miyapur area. Perfect 5 a side for football and 6 a side for box cricket with a special batting pitch in the middle to get the perfect bounce and speed on the wicket. This pitch is unique only to The Turf. There is a Cafe available at venue serving healthy refreshment options as well. Equipment and Washrooms are also available. It's a perfect place to hang out with friends and family, to have get togethers and reunions parties.
